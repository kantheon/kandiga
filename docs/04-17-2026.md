### 07:20 — Resolution: DashScope ≠ local release; precision & voice ruled out

This invalidates comparing Space output as a lower bound.

**User: "people online ran it LOCALLY clean."** Spawned 2nd research agent (20 tool uses, 97s). Findings:
- Zero blog/YouTube/Reddit examples of CPU fp32 clean local runs found
